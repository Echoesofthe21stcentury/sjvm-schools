About SJVM Schools

Sri Jnanakshi Vidya Mandir Schools are dedicated to shaping bright futures through quality education, innovation, and value systems.

Our Vision

We are an Institution of academic excellence teaching and leading excellence, teaching and leading our students to build strong character, courage confidence and intellectual confidence to assume pro active leadership role as global citizens.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that develops every child's multiple intelligence, physical capacities creative potential, 21st century life skills multi cultural awareness and imbibe for universal human values for holistic growth.
